# Health Information Data & Privacy Flow Mapping

## Overview
Mapping of data flows, PHIPA/PIPA roles, and legal agreements 
across a multi-stakeholder regional health system, including 
analysis of a third-party call center engagement scenario.

## Context
Academic project for MHI1001: Information and Communication 
Technology in Healthcare, University of Toronto (2025).

## What Was Mapped
The assignment analysed a regional health system comprising:
- 3 Health Information Custodians (HICs): two hospitals and a 
  primary care clinic
- 1 Health Information Network Provider (HINP): The Health Authority
- 1 Electronic Service Provider (ESP): The Solution Vendor
- Third parties: Shelter Home Network, Caregivers, Call Center

## Deliverables
Three views were produced for both the base scenario and an 
extended call center scenario (6 diagrams total):

- **PHIPA/PIPA Roles View** — classifies each actor by their 
  legal role under Ontario's health privacy legislation
- **Data Flow View** — maps what data moves between which actors 
  and in what direction
- **Legal Agreement View** — maps the governing agreements 
  required at each relationship (Data Sharing & Hosting 
  Agreements, Subscription Agreement, Terms of Use, 
  Explicit Consent)

## Key Concepts Applied
- PHIPA custodian, HINP, and ESP role classification
- Minimum necessary data principle
- Explicit vs. implied consent distinctions
- Third-party data sharing governance
- Role-based access considerations
